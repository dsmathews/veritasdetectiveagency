About Our Agency


Veritas Detective Agency, LLC is a full-service private detective firm. We are licensed and bonded in Georgia. We provide the highest level of expertise and service for each client. You will be assigned a licensed, knowledgeable investigator from your initial consultation to the completion of your investigation.

Together we will develop your objectives and a detailed plan for your case. Veritas Detective Agency will conduct your case with the utmost integrity, professionalism, and discretion. You will be provided concise and timely progress updates throughout your investigation. We will communicate with you in the way you prefer.

Contact Us

Veritas Detective Agency is more than happy to discuss the details of your case, answer questions, and review pricing. To begin this process, call us directly or fill out our online form. Consultations are by appointment only.
