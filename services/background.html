Background Research

A background search is a comprehensive fact check. The report may include, but is not limited to criminal records and superior court checks, litigation and legal matters, education verification, marital information, and employment history. Whether it is or jobs that require a high level of security or if you just want reassurance about someone you welcome into your home, a background check provides the necessary peace of mind. In today’s society you cannot rely on someone’s word alone; you need the facts.

  • Workplace Background Checks

Many companies both large and small choose to perform background checks on potential hires in order to protect their business interests, customers, and staff. Trade secrets and confidential data are shared with employees once they have been hired, and it’s important that employers know who they are trusting with their business and brand. Not everyone is honest on job applications, whether overstating their credentials and experience or attempting to conceal red flags from their past. Pre-employment screening and background checks not only verify if an applicant is being truthful; a thorough screening may reveal a problematic history - saving your organization trouble and financial loss. Enlisting the services of a licensed PI agency to perform thorough background checks is the best way to ensure full transparency throughout the hiring process.

This search includes current and past addresses, current mobile phones, email addresses, landline phones, vehicles, property purchases and sales, current and past acquaintances, relatives, criminal records, assets, and social media.
