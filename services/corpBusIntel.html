Employee Theft

One of the most effective ways to detect and deal with employee theft or malfeasance is through the placement of an undercover investigator inside the affected workplace. This is a method that offers several advantages by having a professional investigator blend in with your employees. It is essential to understand what is involved in undercover investigations and how to maximize their benefit.

Undercover investigators can provide a valuable service in the following situations:

  • Employee Drug Use
  • Industrial Espionage
  • Cyber Security Breaches
  • Product Theft
  • Sexual Harassment
  • Workplace Bullying
  • Shipping/Receiving Fraud
  • Internal/External Bribes
  • Workplace Safety Violations
  • Safety and Security Compliance Issues
  • Workplace Policies Violations
  • Workplace Productivity Issues
