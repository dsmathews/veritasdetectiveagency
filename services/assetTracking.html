Asset Tracking

Asset searches help to uncover whether or not an individual has hidden possessions or belongings such as money, real estate/property, art and jewelry.

Individual asset searches include several types of investigations that a private investigator can help with:

  • Bankruptcies
  • Real Estate
  • Deeds
  • Aircraft Registration and Liens
  • Criminal Records
  • Judgments
  • Vehicle Information
  • Available Mortgage Information
  • Vehicle Registration and Liens
  • Watercraft Registration and Liens
  • A.K.A. (also known as) or D.B.A. (doing business as) of the individual

Corporate asset searches are a little more complex, but a private investigator can help businesses with:

  • Current contact information
  • Corporate associates
  • Federal and State Tax Liens
  • Corporate Filings
  • Liabilities (Judgment, Liens and Bankruptcy Search)
  • Records Search
